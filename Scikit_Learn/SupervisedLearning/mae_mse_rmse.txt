We will judge the model by mistake numbers.
1. MAE - Mean absolute error 
On an avergae model is wrong by how much units
2. MSE - Mean squared error
Here larger error get punished more and small errors dont dominate 
Used when we are looking for larger errors and ignoring the small errors
3. RMSE - Root mean square error
Root of MSE, along with large it also consider small errors.

Use MAE when:
You want simple average error
Outliers not very important
Use MSE when:
You want to heavily punish large errors
Outliers matter
Use RMSE when:
You want squared punishment
But still want understandable unit
